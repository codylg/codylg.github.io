Scrunch

At Scrunch I worked with a small team to build their data-driven influencer marketing platform from the ground up. As lead UI/UX, I oversaw end-to-end feature development from concept to deployment, as well as helping to develop a roadmap of the product direction.

My responsibilities included: user interface & user experience design, responsive web design, front-end development, prototyping, animation & motion design, user testing & analytics, QA/testing, agile development, CSS/Sass, HTML, Git, program management, branding & graphic design.

I also designed and developed the scrunch.com landing page and blog.

iOS'86

iOS'86 was a long-term project that developed into one of the most expansive iPhone themes available. Based on the concept design by Anton Repponen, iOS'86 is the official and fully functional Winterboard theme for iPhone and iPod Touch.

Including thousands of hand-pixeled monochrome graphics along with a port of the original Chicago font; iOS'86 takes your device back to another era. Available on iOS 4, 5, and 6.
